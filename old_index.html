üìã Vendor Purchasing Process

This SOP outlines the standard procedure for purchasing from approved vendors.

1. Purchase Request

  1. Department head submits purchase request via Google Form
  2. Include item description, quantity, estimated cost, and business justification
  3. Attach any relevant quotes or specifications

2. Approval Process

  1. Purchases under $500: Department manager approval
  2. Purchases $500-$5,000: Operations manager approval
  3. Purchases over $5,000: CFO approval required

3. Vendor Selection

  1. Check approved vendor list in AK Purchasing sheet
  2. For new vendors, complete vendor evaluation form
  3. Obtain minimum 3 quotes for purchases over $1,000

4. Purchase Order Creation

  1. Create PO in QuickBooks with approved details
  2. Send PO to vendor via email
  3. Update purchasing tracker with PO number and expected delivery date

üìã Inventory Receiving Process

Standard procedure for receiving and processing inventory deliveries.

1. Delivery Receipt

  1. Verify delivery against purchase order
  2. Check for damaged or missing items
  3. Sign delivery receipt and note any discrepancies

2. Quality Check

  1. Inspect items for quality standards
  2. Take photos of any defects
  3. Complete quality check form

3. System Update

  1. Update Inventory Ahead system with received quantities
  2. Update purchasing tracker status to "Received"
  3. File all documentation in appropriate folder

üìã Invoice Processing

Standard procedure for processing vendor invoices and payments.

1. Invoice Receipt

  1. All invoices should be sent to accounts@company.com
  2. Verify invoice details against PO and delivery receipt
  3. Check for proper approval signatures

2. Invoice Entry

  1. Enter invoice in QuickBooks within 48 hours
  2. Attach digital copy to QuickBooks entry
  3. Code to appropriate expense account

3. Payment Processing

  1. Review payment terms and due dates
  2. Schedule payments for optimal cash flow
  3. Process payments via appropriate method (ACH, wire, check)

üìã Month-End Close Process

Monthly financial closing procedures to ensure accurate reporting.

1. Account Reconciliation

  1. Reconcile all bank accounts by day 3
  2. Reconcile credit card statements
  3. Review and clear suspense accounts

2. Journal Entries

  1. Post standard monthly accruals
  2. Record depreciation entries
  3. Adjust prepaid expenses

3. Financial Reports

  1. Generate P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow statements
  2. Prepare variance analysis
  3. Submit reports to management by day 5
